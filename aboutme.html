About Me:

Hi! I am Darshak Patel. I am a first-year Masters student (Msc of Physics) at the University of Waterloo in the Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics; under the supervison of Dr. Mike Hudson. My research interests include - Star Formation/Evolutiion, Galaxy Formation/Evolution, Dark Matter Halos, Studies of star clusters and galaxy clusters, and Black Holes (AGN).

MSc (2023-2025), University of Waterloo, Astrophysics, Canada

BSc (2018-2023), University of Waterloo, Honours (Co-op) Physics and Astronomy, Canada

Outside of academics, I enjoy staying active. I play a variety of sports recreationally such as; badminton, volleyball, ultimate frisbee, and swimming (just to name a few). I will honestly try and play any sport (atleast once)! My "main sport" however, is Figure Skating. I've been a competitive figure skater for 10+ years starting back in 2010. Before entering University I competed under the Scarboro Figure Skating Club. During my time there I won the Provincial title in the Senior Bronze Mens event at the 2016 Skate Ontario Championships in Sudbury, ON. Since entering university at UWaterloo in 2018, I have been a student-athlete throughout all 5 years of my undergraduate degree part of the Warriors Varsity Figure Skating team. Our 2022/2023 season was our best season since joining the team. At the 2023 Ontario University Association (OUA) Championships I was part of the Bronze medal winning skate in the Dance Fours event! I intend on continuing my skating career with UWaterloo during my masters degree.
